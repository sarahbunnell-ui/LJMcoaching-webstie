About Laurie

A Nurse, a Mother,
and a Lifelong Learner

Laurie has spent 15 years as a registered nurse at a naturopathic cancer healing clinic, but her education in health and wellness began long before that — as a mother of six, driven to understand every aspect of how the human body works and heals.

Her depth of knowledge spans preventative medicine, ancient herbs, supplement interactions, chemical reactions and bodily functions, and the science of how our bodies respond to food, stress, and environment. She doesn't just share information — she helps you understand why it matters for your specific body and life.

Whether you've just received a diagnosis and feel overwhelmed, or you're proactively looking to optimize your health before problems arise, Laurie meets you where you are with compassion, clarity, and expertise.
